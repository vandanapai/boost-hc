Our Approach

Boost Healthcare Consulting is a consulting firm focused on helping healthcare providers identify and recover revenue. Our team works with clients to improve overall revenue by recovering denied and underpaid claims from both commercial and government payors.

Our retrospective claims review is contingency based (no upfront costs) and utilizes our proprietary system, specialized staff, and healthcare expertise to recover revenue. Our approach leverages both technology and people.



We provide clients with claims data showing payer performance and root cause analysis of denial and underpayment issues. This invaluable knowledge is used as leverage when negotiating with payers and assessing overall business performance.
