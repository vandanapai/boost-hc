Our Approach

Boost Healthcare Consulting is a consulting firm focused on helping healthcare providers identify and recover revenue. Our team works with clients to improve overall revenue by recovering denied and underpaid claims from both commercial and government payors.

We provide clients with claims data showing payer performance and root cause analysis of denial and underpayment issues. This invaluable knowledge is used as leverage when negotiating with payers and assessing overall business performance.

Our retrospective claims review is contingency based (no upfront costs) and utilizes our proprietary system, specialized staff, and healthcare expertise to recover revenue. Our approach leverages both technology and people.

Our Team

Boost Healthcare Consulting was founded by Liana Hans in 2013 and has since grown to include a full staff of consultants with a diverse range of backgrounds and experiences.

Liana Hans

Principal and Founder

Liana has more than 17 years of consulting experience focused on payment review and recovery for providers on a national basis. Her background includes identification and recovery of contractual underpayments, claims follow-up, third party appeals, workers' compensation issues, and managed care contracting. She also has experience analyzing the revenue impacts of legislative or contractual changes and developing strategies to mitigate future losses.

Liana is UC Davis alum and an active member of the Healthcare Financial Management Association (HFMA). She is a designated Fellow of HFMA (CHFP/FHFMA Certification) and also holds an HFMA Certificate of Advanced Technical Study in Managed Care.
