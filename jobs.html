Senior Associate Consultant

As a Senior Associate Consultant, you will become an expert in the complex reimbursement methodology between hospitals, and government and private insurance. Your work will focus on identifying missed revenue opportunities, and you will be responsible for conducting analysis, trending, and data mining on large data sets. You will spend time training and developing team members, as well as working directly with clients. You will also be asked to compile and communicate both internal reporting and client reporting. This position requires good time management skills and the ability to be flexible in a professional setting. Candidates should offer a creative and insightful point of view.

This is a great opportunity for anyone with an interest in continuing his/her career in healthcare and managing staff and client engagements.

Desired Qualifications
  • 2 – 5 years of experience
  • Demonstrated leadership positions
  • Strong analytical skills and financial acumen
  • Creative problem solving skills
  • Effective interpersonal and communication skills
  • Team-oriented and flexible

Associate Consultant

As an Associate Consultant, you will become an expert in the complex reimbursement methodology between hospitals and government and private insurance. Your work will focus on identifying missed revenue opportunities, and you will be responsible for conducting analysis, trending, and data mining on large data sets. You will also spend time on the phone calling insurance companies and writing appeal letters to recover lost revenue. You will regularly be asked to conduct research and produce summaries of your findings for management, other team members, and clients. This position requires good time management skills and the ability to be flexible in a professional setting. Candidates should offer a creative and insightful point of view.

The ideal candidate will have a demonstrated interest in healthcare and a desire to strengthen his/her analytical and general consulting skills.

Desired Qualifications
  • Entrepreneurial spirit
  • Undergraduate degree in Public Health, Business, or related field preferred
  • Strong analytical skills and financial acumen
  • Creative problem solving skills
  • Effective interpersonal and communication skills
  • Demonstrated leadership positions
  • Team-oriented and flexible
  • General interest in healthcare
