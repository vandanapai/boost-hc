Senior Consultant

Boost Healthcare Consulting is seeking an execution-oriented and proven individual to assume the role of Senior Consultant. This position is responsible for senior level performance in all areas related to healthcare, auditing, finance, and provision of technical and business solutions to clients. The customer base consists of public and private sector hospitals and clinics.

You must be driven to excellence, have an entrepreneurial spirit and a passion to be a leader in the consulting profession and healthcare industry. We are looking for entry-level professionals and experienced professionals with demonstrated success in healthcare consulting. You must be willing to travel and be able to relocate to within commuting distance of one of our offices. We offer an attractive compensation package that rewards success and accomplishment, as well as the opportunity for rapid professional growth for individuals willing to devote the effort required.


Responsibilities
  • Review, draft, negotiate and manage IO’s and any necessary addendum’s
  • Ensure compliance with regulatory obligations and deal with regulatory bodies as required with a particular focus on healthcare issues
  • To advise on healthcare issues
  • To contribute to the development of best practice with the Finance team
  • Provide guidance on business and legal risks, and company policies, to relevant stakeholders
  • Respond promptly to legal requests from relevant client group
  • Deliver timely, pragmatic, and business-focused legal counsel to your clients, even in ambiguous situations
  • Work effectively with all levels of Boost employees across the organization
  • Build and improve standard form agreements and legal processes


Qualifications
  • Master's Degree – MBA, MHA, MPHA, advanced HR degree
  • 5+ years of experience in either advisory services/consulting in a healthcare provider setting
  • Strong awareness and understanding of the Healthcare industry and major trends in the geography in which client projects are delivered.
  • 2+ years of healthcare clinical or administrative experience
  • Ability to create impactful client facing communications and presentations
  • Adaptable: able to adjust work and communication style based on situational needs.
  • Demonstrate an aptitude for critical thinking to included evaluation of ideas and synthesizing information into insights

Associate Consultant

We are looking for entry-level Associate Consultants who are interested in healthcare, consulting, data analysis, public health, and financial analysis.

Generally, ACs join directly out of college or university or after less than three years of work experience. It's a great way to set yourself up for future success—at Boost and beyond.


Responsibilities
  • Structuring and performing analysis
  • Conducting primary research, to uncover the insights that support our recommendations to clients
  • Presenting at team and client meetings, and determining the most practical way to drive lasting results based on your insights


Qualifications
  • High School Diploma/GED required; Bachelor’s degree in healthcare, business, finance, or related fields preferred
  • Previous internships with consulting experience
  • Strong argumentation and presentation skills required
  • Highly experienced with MS Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
  • Strong interpersonal and team skills
  • Strong analytical and writing skills required for proposal and report development

Consulting Intern

Still in school? We want to support you! We have dynamic opportunities for undergraduate and graduate students in universities. The overall aim of the program is to attract a pool of diverse, skilled, enthusiastic young professionals and provide them with an opportunity to get valuable experience interacting with healthcare companies and hospitals.

This is a paid internship eligible during the school year or Summer 2015 (June 2015 - August 2015). Hours required are 40 hours/week and students are also eligible for earning academic credit.


Responsibilities
  • Develop models to calculate the financial impacts of business scenarios and develop compelling presentations to share with multiple levels of leadership
  • Project engagements may include cost-benefit analyses, scenario testing, benchmarking, and metrics reviews
  • The Intern will work on projects alongside full time Associate Consultants and receive mentorship as well
  • Audit hospitals and healthcare organizations


Qualifications
  • Currently enrolled in an undergraduate or graduate business program with a focus on Biology, Public Health, Business, Finance, Economics, Math or Statistics
  • Advanced computer skills, including Microsoft Office Suite with proficient skills using MS Excel and PowerPoint
  • Ability to communicate complex concepts with clear terminology and professionalism
  • Ability to work in a fast paced environment with a demonstrated ability to accomplish multiple
  • task and meet deadlines
  • Excellent written/oral communication and interpersonal skills are essential
  • Excellent relationship skills and ability to work cross-functionally
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Must be enrolled in a degree program at the time of the internship
