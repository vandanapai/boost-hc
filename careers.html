Your Career

Boost is defined by our insistence on providing our clients with thorough service and unparalleled guidance. All of our team members are charged with bringing creativity, honesty, and intellectual rigor to their responsibilities.

As a member of our team at Boost, you will gain firsthand experience working with healthcare professionals on major business sustainability issues. Team members are assigned to projects based on past experience as well as professional interests, and work together on the revenue recovery process from start to finish. Promising candidates have a demonstrated passion for problem solving and the ability to work effectively with other highly motivated individuals.

Most team members join us as Associate Consultants. Associates work directly with our leadership team and Senior Associates, who manage projects and client relationships. As an Associate, you will learn the foundations of Boost’s coordinated and targeted approach to recovering underpayments and improving overall revenue. After mastering the basics of our model, most Associates continue with Boost as Senior Associates during their second year. Boost believes in fostering everyday learning and professional development to create a team of industry experts and future healthcare leaders.

Life at Boost

Boost is committed to helping you maintain a healthy work-life balance. With a long-term focus on monthly goals and overall company growth, our day-to-day approach is centered around team engagement and personal well-being. As such, we offer our team members the option of a company-sponsored Crossfit membership with workouts integrated into our daily schedule three days a week.

In addition to our comprehensive benefits package, we also host monthly off-site team building events, as well as weekly team lunches and happy hours to further enhance the work environment and promote a balanced professional culture.
