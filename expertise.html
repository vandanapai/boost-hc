Underpayment Recovery

Ensuring you are reimbursed correctly for claims at negotiated rates is essential for the financial health of your organization.

Even with the best processes in place, most providers leave 1% - 5% of net revenue on the table. Boost specializes in working with your internal teams and processes to identify and recover this opportunity in zero-balance and denied claims.

What sets Boost apart is our ability to independently price claims and validate balances. We also provide detailed claims data summarizing the number of claims and dollars assigned, as well as the collection and write-off percentages. We summarize the root cause of the delayed payment or write-off to better help your staff moving forward.

Denial Management

Boost offers denial review services to help you accelerate cash and accurately forecast receivables.

We work to improve overall revenue by achieving payer reimbursement through denial resolution, identifying root cause denial issues, and suggesting permanent solutions to functional defects that cause denials.

Boost will work alongside internal teams to provide detailed appeals for wrongly denied claims and help you recover what is rightfully yours.

Workers’ Compensation

Workers' compensation claims are a problem for many hospitals because they require extensive time and resources as well as the involvement of multiple parties.

Navigating and negotiating with all of these parties requires frequent contact and can place disproportionate demands on hospital staff. Boost specializes in workers' compensation account resolution with a team of expert claims professionals who understand both state payment fee schedules and federal guidelines. Boost takes a hands-on approach by pursuing reimbursement through persistent follow-up and legal-based payment letters to ensure prompt payment.
